About

An operator who advises, not an advisor who reads.

Pinnacle Strategic Advisory is led by Matthew S. Cruz. Founder, Principal, and a working operator running a structural steel fabrication shop in Houston for nine years. The advisory is built around what he has actually lived.

Our Position

We do not advise on problems we have not lived.

Most strategy advice for SMBs is written by people who have never paid a payroll. Never lost a key client on the 25th of the month with a check due on the 30th. Never had to walk into the shop on a Monday and tell the team a job got pulled.

Pinnacle is the opposite. The work is built from nine years of running a structural steel fabrication shop in Houston, bidding against firms ten times our size, and winning because the operating model is tighter, not because we cut corners.

You do not get a junior associate. You get an operator who has run the playbook, with three engagements at a time and a 30-day plan written by hand.

  • i.

    One market, served well.

    Houston SMB owners doing $1M to $50M. We know the problems because we have lived them.

  • ii.

    Principals do the work.

    You hire Matthew. Matthew shows up. No handoffs, no junior pass-through, no team rotation.

  • iii.

    Outcomes, not deliverables.

    The plan is judged by the business you run after we are done. Not the slide count.

  • iv.

    Honest before flattering.

    If your model is broken, we say so. If your strategy is sound, we say that too.
