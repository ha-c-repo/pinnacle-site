The Pinnacle Method

A system, not improvisation.

Every engagement runs on the same four phases. The work changes. The rigor does not.

1

Diagnose

Data, interviews, customer conversations. We map what is actually happening before recommending anything. Weeks 1 to 3.

2

Decide

Working session, not a presentation. Three to five committed initiatives. Owners named. 90-day metrics defined. Weeks 4 to 6.

3

Design

Playbooks, dashboards, and the operating cadence the new direction needs. Weeks 7 to 10.

4

Deploy

Quarterly reviews and direct principal access while the work ships. Months 4 to 12.
